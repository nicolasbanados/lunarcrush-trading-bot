MOMENTUM STRATEGY
OVERVIEW

The Momentum Strategy capitalizes on social media momentum combined with positive sentiment to identify cryptocurrencies experiencing rapid increases in attention and interest. This strategy follows the principle that coins gaining significant social traction often experience corresponding price movements.

HOW IT WORKS

The strategy continuously monitors LunarCrush's social metrics and executes trades when specific conditions are met:

  • Social Volume Spike: Detects when a coin's social mentions increase by at least 25% (adjustable) compared to its recent baseline
  • Positive Sentiment: Confirms that the sentiment score is above 52 (adjustable), indicating bullish community perception
  • Price Correlation: Verifies that social momentum is translating into price action
  • Entry Timing: Opens long positions when both conditions align
KEY PARAMETERS
Social Volume Min Minimum % increase in social mentions required (default: 25%)
Sentiment Min Minimum sentiment score required (default: 52 out of 100)
Leverage Position multiplier (default: 5x)
Stop Loss Maximum loss per trade: -2.5%
Take Profit Target gain per trade: +8%
RISK MANAGEMENT
  • Position Sizing: Uses 40% of total capital allocation
  • Tight Stops: 2.5% stop loss to limit downside on false signals
  • Quick Exits: Designed for fast-moving trades (2-8 hour duration)
  • High Frequency: Generates 8-12 signals per day with current settings
BEST USE CASES
  • Bull markets with high retail participation
  • News-driven events (partnerships, launches, listings)
  • Trending topics on crypto social media
  • Meme coin pumps and viral narratives
ADJUSTING SENSITIVITY

Lower sensitivity (15-20% social volume): Generates more signals but includes weaker momentum plays. Use in quiet markets.

Higher sensitivity (35-50% social volume): Generates fewer but higher-quality signals. Use in volatile markets to avoid false breakouts.

ALTRANK STRATEGY
OVERVIEW

The AltRank Strategy identifies cryptocurrencies experiencing significant improvements in their AltRank™ score. AltRank is LunarCrush's proprietary metric that ranks coins based on a combination of social activity, market performance, and overall market influence. Rising AltRank often precedes price appreciation.

HOW IT WORKS

This strategy monitors rank changes across the cryptocurrency universe and takes positions when coins show strong upward rank momentum:

  • Rank Tracking: Monitors AltRank position for top 150 coins (adjustable)
  • Improvement Detection: Identifies coins that have improved their rank by at least 10 positions (adjustable) in recent period
  • Quality Filter: Ensures the coin is within top 150 to avoid low-liquidity assets
  • Breakout Confirmation: Verifies that rank improvement is sustained, not a temporary spike
KEY PARAMETERS
Rank Improve Min Minimum rank positions gained required (default: 10)
Top Coins Maximum rank to consider (default: 150)
Leverage Position multiplier (default: 4x)
Stop Loss Maximum loss per trade: -3%
Take Profit Target gain per trade: +10%
RISK MANAGEMENT
  • Position Sizing: Uses 30% of total capital allocation
  • Moderate Stops: 3% stop loss allows for normal volatility
  • Medium Duration: Trades typically last 4-12 hours
  • Selective Entry: Generates 3-6 signals per day with current settings
WHAT IS ALTRANK?

AltRank™ is a composite score that considers:

  • Social Volume: Total mentions across social platforms
  • Social Engagement: Likes, shares, comments on social posts
  • Social Sentiment: Overall positive/negative perception
  • Market Cap: Relative size and liquidity
  • Price Performance: Recent price momentum

A coin improving from rank #80 to #70 indicates growing market influence and attention, which often precedes price increases.

BEST USE CASES
  • Identifying emerging narratives before they go mainstream
  • Catching altcoins rotating into favor
  • Trading sector rotations (DeFi, Gaming, AI, etc.)
  • Finding coins with improving fundamentals
ADJUSTING SENSITIVITY

Lower sensitivity (5-8 rank improvement, 180-200 top coins): Casts a wider net, includes smaller rank jumps and lower-ranked coins. More signals but lower quality.

Higher sensitivity (15-20 rank improvement, 80-100 top coins): Only trades significant rank breakouts in top-tier coins. Fewer but higher-conviction signals.

REVERSAL STRATEGY
OVERVIEW

The Reversal Strategy is a contrarian approach that identifies high-quality cryptocurrencies experiencing temporary price dips. It buys the dip when strong social fundamentals suggest the decline is an overreaction rather than a fundamental breakdown. This "buy low" strategy targets mean reversion in quality assets.

HOW IT WORKS

The strategy waits for short-term price weakness in fundamentally strong coins, then enters positions expecting a bounce:

  • Price Drop Detection: Identifies coins that have dropped at least 3% (adjustable) in recent period
  • Quality Filter: Requires Galaxy Score above 65 (adjustable) to ensure social fundamentals remain strong
  • Divergence Check: Confirms that social metrics (volume, engagement) haven't collapsed with price
  • Entry Timing: Opens long positions when price is down but social strength persists
KEY PARAMETERS
Price Drop Min Minimum % price decline required (default: -3%)
Galaxy Score Min Minimum quality score required (default: 65 out of 100)
Leverage Position multiplier (default: 3x)
Stop Loss Maximum loss per trade: -4%
Take Profit Target gain per trade: +12%
RISK MANAGEMENT
  • Position Sizing: Uses 20% of total capital allocation
  • Wider Stops: 4% stop loss accommodates volatility during reversals
  • Longer Duration: Trades may take 6-24 hours to reach targets
  • Conservative Frequency: Generates 2-4 signals per day with current settings
WHAT IS GALAXY SCORE?

Galaxy Score™ is LunarCrush's overall quality metric that combines:

  • Social Strength: Consistency and growth of social presence
  • Community Health: Engagement quality and sentiment trends
  • Market Position: Liquidity, volume, and market cap stability
  • Price Stability: Historical volatility and drawdown patterns

A high Galaxy Score (65+) indicates a coin with strong fundamentals that is likely to recover from temporary price weakness.

BEST USE CASES
  • Market-wide corrections affecting quality assets
  • Temporary FUD (Fear, Uncertainty, Doubt) events
  • Stop-loss cascades creating oversold conditions
  • End-of-day profit-taking in strong coins
STRATEGY PHILOSOPHY

Unlike momentum strategies that "buy high and sell higher," this strategy "buys low and sells normal." It exploits the market's tendency to overreact to short-term price movements while ignoring strong underlying fundamentals. The key insight: if social metrics remain strong during a price dip, the dip is likely temporary.

ADJUSTING SENSITIVITY

Lower sensitivity (-2% drop, 55-60 galaxy score): Buys smaller dips in a wider range of coins. More signals but includes weaker reversals.

Higher sensitivity (-6% to -8% drop, 70-75 galaxy score): Only buys significant dips in the highest-quality coins. Fewer signals but stronger conviction.
